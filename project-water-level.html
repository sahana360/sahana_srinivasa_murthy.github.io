Overview

Smart water management through IoT threshold alerting

Water tank overflow is a persistent and overlooked source of water wastage — particularly in tall buildings where the tank is elevated and difficult to monitor visually. Individuals, already managing professional and household responsibilities, cannot continuously watch water levels manually.

This project creates and deploys a water detection alarm system using the Arduino UNO microcontroller board. The system monitors water levels in overhead tanks and emits audible and visual alarms when the tank reaches capacity — automatically signalling overflow prevention in real time.

The device contributes to the global Save Water campaign by providing a low-cost, compact, easily installable solution that prevents overflow, reduces water wastage, and enables remote pump control.

Problem Statement

Tank overflow — a preventable waste problem

Water tank overflow poses a common challenge in modern buildings, particularly where tanks are placed at height. Without continuous monitoring, tanks fill beyond capacity and overflow — wasting water resources and potentially causing structural damage through wall and roof seepage.

The absence of an automated alert mechanism means users either forget to switch off pumps or cannot tell when the tank has reached full capacity from a distance.

Solution Approach

The system uses water-level sensing probes placed at predetermined thresholds within the tank (25%, 50%, 75%, and full). Analog signals from sensors are read by the Arduino UNO microcontroller via its ADC (Analog-to-Digital Converter) pins.

When the water reaches full capacity, the microcontroller triggers: an audio buzzer alarm to alert the user and displays the fill percentage on an LCD screen. Additionally, the system supports automated pump shutoff when the full threshold is reached, preventing overflow without manual intervention.

System Architecture

The system follows a straightforward sensing → processing → output architecture built around the Arduino UNO as the central microcontroller.

Applications

Deployable across residential and industrial settings

01
Tall Buildings & High-Rise Structures
The primary application — where overhead tank placement makes visual monitoring impossible. The system provides real-time status without requiring the user to physically check the tank.
02
Automated Pump Control
The control panel can be programmed to automatically activate or deactivate a water pump when levels drop below or rise above defined thresholds, enabling fully autonomous water management.
03
Hotels, Pools & Factories
Commercial environments with large storage tanks or pools benefit from automated monitoring, reducing the need for manual rounds and preventing costly overflow events.
04
Fire Protection Systems
Fire suppression water storage must be maintained at sufficient levels at all times. The alarm system ensures maintenance teams are alerted when reserves drop below safe thresholds.
